# Privacy Policy — Tab Group Manager

**Effective date:** 2026-05-06

## Summary

Tab Group Manager does **not** collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All data stays on your device.

## Data Stored Locally

The extension stores the following data in `chrome.storage.local` (on your device only):

| Data | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Tab group names and colors | Display and restore your groups |
| Tab titles and URLs within groups | Enable search across groups |
| Categories and notes you add | Organize groups |
| Archived group history | Restore previously closed groups |

This data never leaves your browser and is never sent to any server.

## Permissions Used

| Permission | Why |
|---|---|
| `tabGroups` | Read tab group names, colors, and state to keep the list up to date |
| `tabs` | Read tab titles and URLs to display them in the popup and enable search |
| `storage` | Save group data locally on your device |
| `bookmarks` | Save a tab group as a Chrome bookmark when you choose to |

## No Data Collection

- No analytics or tracking
- No remote servers or external requests
- No advertising
- No data shared with third parties
